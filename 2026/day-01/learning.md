# Day 1 - DevOps Learning Journey

## Q1. What is your understanding of DevOps?

DevOps is a process, culture, and mindset that combines Development and Operations.

In this process, developers build applications, and those applications are tested and deployed into production efficiently. DevOps helps improve collaboration, automation, and delivery speed while reducing manual effort.
